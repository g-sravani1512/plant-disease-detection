      Frequently Asked Questions

      Everything you need to know about PlantCare AI

      How accurate is the AI detection?

      Our system achieves over 95% accuracy in detecting plant diseases, trained on thousands of real-world plant images.

      What types of plants are supported?

      We currently support 25+ common plant diseases across vegetables, fruits, and ornamental plants including tomatoes, apples, grapes, and more.

      Is my data private and secure?

      Yes! Your uploaded images are processed securely and are not stored permanently. We respect your privacy.

      Can I use this offline?

      Currently, PlantCare AI requires an internet connection for AI processing. Offline capabilities are coming soon!

      How do I get the best results?

      Use clear, well-lit photos of individual leaves showing symptoms. Follow our "Tips for Best Results" section for guidance.

      What if the diagnosis is wrong?

      While our AI is highly accurate, we recommend consulting with a local agricultural expert for final confirmation and treatment.
